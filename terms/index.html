the short version

broqué is a free, for-fun budgeting app. It runs in your browser and stores your data there. We make no promises about your finances, your taste, or your restraint. Use it, enjoy it, don’t sue us.

What broqué is

broqué is a personal budgeting tool with opinions. You enter what you spend; it shows you progress bars and gentle judgment. That’s the whole product. It is not financial advice, accounting software, or a substitute for actually checking your bank account.

Your data, your browser

In this version, everything you enter is stored locally in your browser via localStorage. We don’t have a copy. If you clear your browser data, switch devices, or use a different browser, your ledger does not follow you. Export to CSV from the dashboard if you want a backup.

Acceptable use

  • Be honest with yourself about the dining-out number. Or don’t. We can’t see it either way.
  • Don’t try to use broqué to do anything illegal, which would be a strange choice for a budgeting app.
  • The snark is affectionate. Please do not take spending advice from a progress bar literally.

broqué is provided “as is,” with all its quirks. We make no guarantee that it is accurate, available, bug-free, or that it will improve your financial situation. It may, however, make you feel slightly more seen.
