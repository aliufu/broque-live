the short version

We collect your email and the spending you log. We don't store card numbers, bank credentials, or anything we don't need to run the app. You can delete your account and your data goes with it. We don't sell anything to anyone.

Who this is

broqué is operated as a personal project. This policy describes what happens to information you give us when you use the app at broque.app. If you have questions, email hello@broque.app.

What we collect

  • Your email address. Used to send you a magic-link sign-in. Nothing else.
  • Transaction data you provide. Merchant names, amounts, dates, and categories — either typed in manually or extracted from statement files you upload.
  • Budget settings. The categories and limits you set up.
  • Basic technical logs. IP address and request timestamps, kept short-term for security and debugging. Not joined to your identity except when investigating an actual issue.

What we don't collect

  • Credit or debit card numbers.
  • Bank login credentials. broqué does not connect to your bank. There is no Plaid integration.
  • Social security numbers or government IDs.
  • Physical addresses, phone numbers, or birthdates.
  • Browsing data outside broqué, third-party tracking pixels, or advertising IDs.

Statement uploads

When you upload a PDF, CSV, or Excel statement for parsing, the statement text is sent to Anthropic's API (the maker of Claude) so the AI can extract transactions. Before sending, we redact strings of nine or more consecutive digits (account and card numbers). Anthropic's policy on API data is at anthropic.com/legal/privacy; per their terms, API inputs are not used to train their models. We don't keep the raw statement file after parsing — only the extracted transactions you choose to import.

How we use what we collect

  • To run the app: show your transactions, calculate budgets, and persist your data between sessions.
  • To sign you in via magic-link email.
  • To investigate bugs and security issues.

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't use it for training AI models.

Who we share data with

To run broqué we use a few standard infrastructure providers, each of which sees only what they need:

  • Supabase — hosts the database where your transactions and budgets live.
  • Vercel — hosts the website and runs the server-side code.
  • Anthropic — parses your uploaded statements, as described above.

We don't share your data with anyone else. We will respond to lawful legal requests if we have to, but we'll push back on overreach where we can.

How long we keep your data

As long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your transactions, budgets, and email are removed within 30 days. Backups roll off within 60 days after that. Anonymized aggregate logs (no identifying info) may persist longer for security analysis.

Your rights

  • Access — see what we have on you. Email us and we'll send it.
  • Export — download your transactions as CSV any time from the app.
  • Delete — delete your account from settings, or email us to do it for you.
  • Correct — fix any of your data directly in the app.

If you live in California, the EU, or anywhere with stronger protections, those rights apply to you too. The list above already covers them.

Cookies

We use a small number of cookies needed to keep you signed in (session cookies set by Supabase auth). No advertising cookies, no tracking pixels.

Children

broqué is not intended for users under 13. If we discover an account belongs to a child, we'll delete it.

Security

Your data sits behind row-level security in our database, meaning queries are filtered by user identity at the database layer — even our own code can't see another user's transactions without proper authentication. Connections are encrypted in transit (HTTPS everywhere). No system is perfectly secure, but these are the basics done right.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll email anyone with an active account before it takes effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page tracks revisions.

Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns: hello@broque.app.
